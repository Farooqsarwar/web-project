"Experience the true essence of Shinwari cuisine. At Khyber Shinwari Meat, we bring you the authentic flavors of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region, renowned for its rich culinary heritage."

  • We prioritize the use of the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our meats are carefully selected and expertly prepared to ensure an unforgettable dining experience.
  • Our menu features a wide array of traditional Shinwari dishes, passed down through generations. From succulent kebabs and flavorful curries to aromatic biryanis and delectable desserts, we offer a taste of true Pakistani culinary artistry.
  • Whether you're looking for a relaxing dine-in experience or a convenient take-out option, Khyber Shinwari Meat is the perfect place to savor the flavors of Pakistan.

At Khyber Shinwari, we believe that dining is more than just a meal. We strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can enjoy authentic Pakistani cuisine with friends and family.
